Project Idea

Overview

The project that I would like to undertake is a mobile application. It will be an app that allows the user to calculate transport costs, check spreadsheets on machinery and have the contact information to speak to a representative if further help is needed. This app would be used for customers to find the equipment they require; they then can look at the cost of the unit they were looking at. They can then calculate the transport pricing to wherever they need it to go. Employees can also use the app to calculate the same information for orders or quotes received over the phone.

Motivation

With the creation of this mobile application, it will bring ease to a hire controller's job; with this application, they will be able to calculate what the transport charges will be without needing to google the distance and find out machine specs such as weight calculate the cost. This application will streamline the process for customers as they will be able to access all the necessary information in one place from anywhere they would like; there will be no need for multiple phone calls or emails to the hire controllers to get the required information they need to put together a quote.

Project Description

The mobile application will be an all-in-one quotation/ordering tool that allows customers to search for the information they require while also allowing them to place orders for the equipment they need on-site. The app's first feature would be that customers could access machinery spec sheets and look at the necessary technical data required to use the unit on-site. Using the data obtained for the spec sheets, the customer could then liaise with the site engineer to get approval for the machinery needed on-site as specific machines would be too heavy or too wide for the required environment. The app's second feature would be a transportation calculator that would allow customers to search the suburb they require the machinery to be carted to. This calculator will show the customer the travel distance from our main branch; this will only extend to a 250klm radius; once that distance is exceeded, they will be required to call a hire controller to obtain a transportation quote. It will then show the customer whether toll charges are necessary for the delivery and pick. The customer will then be shown what size truck will be needed to cart the machinery based on the unit’s technical data from the spec sheets. And lastly, the customer will then be shown the total cost of transport to the suburb the customer supplied. The app's third feature would be that we could customise it to each customer by having logins; the customer would then be able to access their rate cards created by their sales representative. The rate card will show the customer the daily, weekly and transport rate on each piece of machinery. The transport charge would be a standard local transport charge; once 45kms is exceeded, the customer will need to use the transport calculator function. The app's fourth feature would be that the customer could place their order through the app instead of ringing the hire controllers to place the order. This feature will be in the form of drop-down boxes and text boxes. The drop-down boxes will allow them to choose the standard information such as date, time, and type of machinery, etcetera; the text boxes will be used for more complex answers such as the delivery address and contact number for the person taking collection of the machinery. Once the order is placed, it will send it through in an email to the hire controllers to process, and it will also send through a confirmation email to the customer. The app's last feature will a contact us page with all contact details linked to the customer's account. There would be a number for the customer to call if there were any issues with the application not working correctly or any malfunctions with the app. This feature will also allow customers to contact hire controllers with any technical questions about anything they did not understand on the spec sheets. They will also be able to contact the sales representative looking after their companies account about any questions to do with their hire rates and negotiate the pricing if required.
